Lane Goodman is a designer focused on helping mission-driven organizations and brands elevate their visual design and online experiences to inspire action and advocate for social change

My background is in photography and design, and I have over six years of experience promoting brands and missions. I am enthusiastic about how good design and new technologies can increase awareness of and advocacy for global development challenges. I currently work on web strategy and graphic design at Results for Development where I also provide graphic design and web platform guidance for the organization.

I served as a public health educator in Guinea with the Peace Corps, where I worked on malaria prevention, reproductive health education and infrastructure management. My previous experience includes working in the fashion photography industry in New York City, teaching photography and English in Andhra Pradesh, India, and leading environmental education voyages with the Hudson River Sloop Clearwater.


I hold a BFA in photography and a BA in anthropology, both from Washington University in St. Louis.
