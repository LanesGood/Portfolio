Hello, I'm Lane

My background is in photography and design. I am enthusiastic about how good design and new technologies can increase awareness of and advocacy for global development challenges. I currently work on web strategy and graphic design at Results for Development where I also provide web platform guidance for the organization. I have over six years of experience promoting brands and missions.

I served as a public health educator in Guinea with the Peace Corps, where I worked on malaria prevention, reproductive health education and infrastructure management. My previous experience includes working in the fashion photography industry in New York City, teaching photography and English in Andhra Pradesh, India, and leading environmental education voyages with the Hudson River Sloop Clearwater.

I hold a BFA in photography and a BA in anthropology, both from Washington University in St. Louis. I have a hard time saying what my favorite book is, because they're all my favorite.
