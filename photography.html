Guinea

I served as a Peace Corps volunteer in Guinea, West Africa, from 2011 to 2014. Most of these images are of the people of the village where I lived for those 2+ years. Sanguiana is a small rural village in the dusty region of Upper Guinea where people live off the land, families are close, and human ingenuity and celebration are visible every day.
